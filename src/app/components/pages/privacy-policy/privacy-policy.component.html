Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Who We Are

V4 Entertainments ("we", "us", "our") is an event management company based in the United Kingdom, specialising in South Indian and Malayalam music events. Our website is v4entertainments.co.uk.

We are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following personal data:

  • Contact information — name, email address, phone number when you enquire or book with us.
  • Transaction data — details of ticket purchases and payments made through our ticketing partners.
  • Usage data — information about how you use our website, collected via cookies and analytics tools.
  • Communications — records of your correspondence with us via phone, email or WhatsApp.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:

  • Process bookings and ticket purchases.
  • Respond to your enquiries and provide customer support.
  • Send you event updates and newsletters (only if you have opted in).
  • Improve our website and services through analytics.
  • Comply with our legal obligations.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract — processing necessary to fulfil a booking or purchase.
  • Legitimate interests — improving our services and communicating with customers.
  • Consent — for marketing communications, which you can withdraw at any time.
  • Legal obligation — where required by law.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with trusted third parties solely to operate our business, including:

  • Ticketing platforms and payment processors.
  • Venue partners for event operations.
  • IT and analytics service providers.

All third parties are required to process your data lawfully and securely.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. Booking records are typically retained for 6 years for accounting purposes.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict processing.
  • Data portability.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@v4entertainments.co.uk.

8. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyse site traffic. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.
